Frequently
Asked Questions

Common questions about sober living at NewBridge Living

What are the requirements to live at NewBridge Living?

We require completion of a treatment program (inpatient or outpatient) and at least 30 days of sobriety. Each application is reviewed individually to ensure a good fit for our community.

What does it cost to live at NewBridge Living?

Monthly rent covers housing, utilities, internet, and basic amenities. Please contact us at (850) 555-0123 for current rates and payment options.

What are the house rules and expectations?

We maintain a 100% drug and alcohol-free environment with regular drug testing, curfew guidelines, shared household responsibilities, and respect for fellow residents. Our supportive community focuses on accountability and growth.

What support is available to residents?

We provide 24/7 house management support, peer support from fellow residents, life skills development, job placement assistance, and connections to local recovery resources and support groups.
