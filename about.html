About NewBridge Living

Creating supportive recovery housing where individuals rebuild their lives with dignity and develop the foundation for lasting independence.

Our Story

NewBridge Living was founded on the belief that every individual deserves a safe, supportive environment to rebuild their life and achieve lasting recovery.

Our Commitment

At NewBridge Living, we provide more than just housing – we offer a comprehensive support system designed to help individuals transition from treatment to independent living. Our approach focuses on building life skills, fostering community connections, and creating a foundation for long-term success.

Our recovery housing serves as a crucial step in the recovery journey. We understand that the transition from structured treatment to independent living can be challenging, which is why we provide comprehensive support, peer connections, and access to local resources.

Our mission extends beyond providing shelter – we're committed to helping each resident develop the tools, confidence, and support network necessary for sustained recovery and personal growth.

Making a Difference

Since our founding, we've been dedicated to providing quality recovery housing that makes a real impact in people's lives.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, supportive, and structured environment that empowers individuals in recovery to rebuild their lives with dignity, develop essential life skills, and successfully transition to independent living.

What We Believe

Recovery is a journey that requires more than just abstaining from substances. It requires rebuilding one's life from the ground up, developing healthy relationships, learning essential life skills, and creating a foundation for long-term success.

At NewBridge Living, we provide the bridge between structured treatment and independent living. Our residents benefit from comprehensive support, peer connections, life skills development, and access to community resources that support their recovery journey.

We are committed to treating every individual with dignity and respect, recognizing that each person's path to recovery is unique and deserves compassionate support.

Our Values

These core values guide everything we do at NewBridge Living

Dignity

Every individual deserves to be treated with respect, understanding, and compassion regardless of their past.

Stability

Providing a secure, consistent environment where residents can focus on their recovery and personal growth.

Growth

Supporting each resident's journey toward independence through skill building and personal development.

Community

Fostering connections and peer support that extend beyond residence to create lasting recovery networks.
