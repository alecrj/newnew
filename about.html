About NewBridge Living

Providing dignity, stability, and growth as a bridge to independence in Tallahassee, Florida.

Our Story

NewBridge Living was founded on the belief that every individual deserves a safe, supportive environment to rebuild their life and achieve lasting recovery.

Our Commitment

At NewBridge Living, we provide more than just housing – we offer a comprehensive support system designed to help individuals transition from treatment to independent living. Our approach focuses on building life skills, fostering community connections, and creating a foundation for long-term success.

Located in Tallahassee, Florida, our recovery housing serves as a crucial step in the recovery journey. We understand that the transition from structured treatment to independent living can be challenging, which is why we provide 24/7 house management, peer support, and connections to local resources.

Our mission extends beyond providing shelter – we're committed to helping each resident develop the tools, confidence, and support network necessary for sustained recovery and personal growth.
